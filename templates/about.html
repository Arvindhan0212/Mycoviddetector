About MyCovidDetector

This website helps in predicting the risk of having COVID-19 based on your symptoms and other factors. It utilizes two models:

  • Ensemble Model: A combination of multiple machine learning algorithms to improve the prediction accuracy. By aggregating the strengths of different models, the ensemble approach provides more reliable predictions.
  • DBSCAN Clustering: A density-based clustering algorithm that groups similar data points and identifies outliers to predict the risk. This method is particularly useful for finding patterns in complex datasets and recognizing atypical cases.

By leveraging these advanced techniques, MyCovidDetector aims to offer a useful tool for assessing COVID-19 risk, helping individuals make informed decisions about their health.
